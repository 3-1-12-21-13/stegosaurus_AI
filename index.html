Stegosaurus Facts

  • Stegosaurus was a plant-eating dinosaur that lived during the Jurassic period, around 150 million years ago.
  • It was around 30 feet long and weighed up to 6,800 pounds.
  • The most distinctive feature of the Stegosaurus was the row of bony plates along its back and tail, which may have been used for display or temperature regulation.
  • It also had a spiked tail, which could be swung at predators as a defensive weapon.
