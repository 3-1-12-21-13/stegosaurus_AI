Meet the Stegosaurus

The stegosaurus is one of the most recognizable dinosaurs, known for its distinctive plates and spikes.

Learn More

Fun Facts

  • The stegosaurus lived during the Late Jurassic period, around 155 to 150 million years ago.
  • The plates on the stegosaurus's back were likely used for display and thermoregulation.
  • The stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut, one of the smallest of any dinosaur.
